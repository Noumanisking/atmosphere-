Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our membership packages

Is there a joining fee?

Yes, there is a one-time joining fee of Rs. 2,000 for all new members. This fee is waived during certain promotional periods and for yearly membership commitments.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for a period of time depending on your membership tier. Basic members can freeze for up to 30 days per year, Premium for 60 days, and Elite for 90 days. A small administrative fee may apply.
