Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Atmosphere

What are your operating hours?

Our standard operating hours are Monday to Saturday from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM, and Sunday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. The female-only gym has special hours: Monday to Saturday from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

How do I become a member?

You can become a member by registering online through our website or by visiting any of our branches in person. Our staff will guide you through the membership options and help you choose the best package for your needs.

Do you offer free trials?

Yes, we offer a one-day free trial for new visitors. You can register for a free trial on our website or by visiting any of our branches. This allows you to experience our facilities and services before committing to a membership.

What amenities are included with membership?

Our standard membership includes access to all gym equipment, locker rooms, and shower facilities. Depending on your membership tier, you may also get access to group classes, personal training sessions, the rejuvenation zone, and other premium amenities.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for a period of 15 to 90 days per year, depending on your membership type. A small administrative fee may apply. Please contact our customer service team or visit the reception desk at your branch to arrange this.
