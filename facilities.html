Our Facilities

State-of-the-art equipment in a futuristic training environment

World-Class Facilities

Experience fitness reimagined with our premium facilities designed for optimal performance

Cardio Section

Equipped with the latest treadmills, ellipticals, bikes, and rowing machines, our cardio section features integrated entertainment systems and performance tracking.

Strength Area

Our strength zone features premium free weights, machines, and functional training equipment from top global brands designed for both beginners and advanced athletes.

Functional Fit

Our dedicated functional training space includes kettlebells, battle ropes, slam balls, TRX systems, and open floor areas for dynamic movement training.

Studio

Our spacious studio features floating floors, mirror walls, advanced sound systems, and customizable lighting for a variety of group classes and specialized training.

Boxing

Our boxing zone includes professional-grade punching bags, speed bags, a boxing ring, and training equipment for both beginners and advanced boxers.

Rejuvenation

Our recovery zone features massage chairs, foam rollers, compression therapy systems, and stretching areas to help you recover faster and perform better.

Cricket Academy

Our specialized cricket training facility includes batting nets, bowling machines, and professional coaching staff to develop all aspects of your cricket skills.

Archery

Our archery range offers multiple target distances, professional equipment rental, and expert instruction for beginners and experienced archers alike.

Football Academy

Our football training facility includes indoor futsal courts, skill development areas, and professional coaching for players of all ages and skill levels.
